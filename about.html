About SMB AI Stack

SMB AI Stack is an independent review site for solopreneurs and small business owners who want to use AI tools without the hype. We write because we use these tools ourselves, and because the gap between vendor marketing and what actually works is bigger than it should be.

What we cover

We focus on tools that fit a 1-10 person business: AI writers, AI agents, no-code platforms, email marketing, CRMs, automation. We avoid enterprise-only tools because the buyers they target are different and the pricing is different.

How we keep the lights on

We earn affiliate commissions when you buy a product through our links. See our full disclosure for the list of programs we participate in. No one has ever paid us for a review and no one ever will.
