Start with the first two, add the third, then iterate.

Best 3 to start with: Systeme.io (run your funnel + email), Notion AI (your knowledge + AI), Writesonic (your copy + blog). Total: $57/mo.

How to deploy this stack

Step 1: Systeme.io free tier. Build one funnel (lead magnet → landing page → thank-you → email welcome sequence). This is your sales engine.

Step 2: Notion free tier. Start your content calendar, SOPs, and client docs. Run for 2 weeks. Then upgrade to Notion AI ($10/mo) when you start using AI weekly.

Step 3: Writesonic free tier. Generate 4 blog posts. If you publish more than 4 articles/month, upgrade to Writesonic Pro at $20/mo. If brand voice matters more than volume, switch to Jasper instead.

Step 4 (after first paying customer): add ConvertKit if your list grew beyond Systeme's free tier. Otherwise, stay on Systeme and skip ConvertKit.

Step 5 (after $1k monthly revenue): add a dedicated social tool (Predis or Brandwell) and Zapier for the automations you cannot do natively.

What to skip

  • Don't subscribe to Jasper AND Writesonic. Pick one based on use case.
  • Don't subscribe to ConvertKit AND beehiiv. Pick based on product shape: ConvertKit = funnel + email; beehiiv = newsletter-first.
  • Skip ClickFunnels and Kartra at this stage — Systeme is cheaper and does enough.

The 80/20 plan: $30/mo total

If even $57/mo feels heavy, drop to: Notion AI ($10) + Writesonic ($20) + Systeme.io free. Total: $30/mo. Skip email marketing as a dedicated tool — write emails manually in Notion, use Systeme's free tier for the funnel, and deploy as you grow.
