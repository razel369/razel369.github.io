Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This site is a participant in affiliate programs for some of the products we recommend. When you click a link on SMB AI Stack and buy a product, we may earn a commission. There is no extra cost to you. Affiliate commissions are how this site stays free and independent.

How this site monetizes (the 3-network structure)

To keep signups to a minimum, this site participates through three affiliate networks plus a handful of direct programs:

  1. PartnerStack — one approval covers Notion (50% Y1), beehiiv (50% recurring), Jasper (25-30%), Webflow (50%), Pipedrive (33%).
  2. Skimlinks — JS auto-monetizer. Any outbound link to a SaaS company in their catalog becomes a monetized affiliate redirect at click-time, no per-program signup required.
  3. Impact — network covering Canva, ConvertKit, HubSpot, Semrush, ActiveCampaign.
  4. Direct programs — Systeme.io (40-60% lifetime), GoHighLevel (40% lifetime), Writesonic, Hostinger (60%/sale).

The user maintains this list with a single file edit (/affiliates.json). No KYC, no per-program approval, no per-article code change.

How this affects our reviews

Affiliate programs do not change our verdicts. We do not accept payment for placement, ranked position, or to soften a weakness. If a tool deserves a 1-star callout for a missing feature, we say so. If a tool is great, we say so regardless of commission rate. Programs on this page are listed in payout order; this does not affect ranking position within articles.

Editorial independence

We never guarantee a positive review to a partner. We sometimes cover tools we do not yet promote because the affiliate program terms do not fit small-business audiences (e.g., enterprise-only, restricted verticals). When this changes, we update this page.

How we test

All recommendations on this site are based on one of the following:

  1. Direct hands-on use by our reviewers for at least 14 days
  2. Public benchmarks and pricing data verified within the last 60 days
  3. Aggregated verified user reviews (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, AppSumo reviews)
