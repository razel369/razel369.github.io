Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This site is a participant in affiliate programs for some of the products we recommend. When you click a link on SMB AI Stack and buy a product, we may earn a commission. There is no extra cost to you. Affiliate commissions are how this site stays free and independent.

What programs we participate in

  • Systeme.io — 40-60% lifetime recurring (highest-paying program we recommend)
  • Notion — 50% on first 12 months of referred customers (via PartnerStack)
  • beehiiv — 50% recurring on newsletter platform plans
  • Jasper AI — 25% base, 30% top-tier recurring for 12 months
  • Writesonic — 20-30% recurring commission
  • ConvertKit (Kit) — 30% recurring for 24 months
  • GoHighLevel — 40% lifetime recurring + 5% tier 2 override
  • Webflow — 50% recurring (via PartnerStack)

How this affects our reviews

Affiliate programs do not change our verdicts. We do not accept payment for placement, ranked position, or to soften a weakness. If a tool deserves a 1-star callout for a missing feature, we say so. If a tool is great, we say so regardless of commission rate. Programs on this page are listed alphabetically — there is no preference for higher-commission programs in our rankings.

Editorial independence

We never guarantee a positive review to a partner. We sometimes cover tools we do not yet promote because the affiliate program terms do not fit small-business audiences (e.g., enterprise-only, restricted verticals). When this changes, we update this page.

How we test

All recommendations on this site are based on one of the following:

  1. Direct hands-on use by our reviewers for at least 14 days
  2. Public benchmarks and pricing data verified within the last 60 days
  3. Aggregated verified user reviews (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, AppSumo reviews)

Questions?

Open an issue on our GitHub repository if you spot an error, want us to test a tool, or have a correction to suggest.
